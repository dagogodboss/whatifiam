Investor Relations

Signature Bank® (Nasdaq:SBNY), founded in 2001, is a New York-based full-service commercial bank with 30 private client offices located in the New York metropolitan area. The Bank serves the needs of privately owned business clients, their owners and senior managers. Signature Bank offers a wide variety of business and personal banking products and services through the bank as well as investment, brokerage, asset management and insurance products and services through our subsidiary, Signature Securities Group Corporation, a licensed broker-dealer and investment adviser.

This seamless, best-of-breed financial services solution is delivered through a private client group structure focused on combining smaller bank service levels and responsiveness with large bank experience and capabilities.

Signature Bank intends to continue its growth, to become a premier relationship-based financial services organization in the New York metropolitan area.
