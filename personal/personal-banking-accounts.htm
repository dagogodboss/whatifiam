[1] The Monogram Insured Money Market Account and Monogram Money Market Funds Program Accounts allow for limited “pre-authorized” withdrawals. Pre-authorized withdrawals are withdrawals made by check, debit card payment to third parties, telephone, fax, withdrawal transfers via the Internet or instructions to a creditor. Withdrawals or transfers initiated at an ATM are not pre-authorized, and are unlimited. Exceeding 6 pre-authorized withdrawals per monthly statement cycle will lead to a $15 per item fee on the Insured Money Market Account and a $25 per cycle fee on the Money Market Funds Program Accounts. In addition to this fee the Monogram Insured Money Market Account may be closed or converted to an account type which is not subject to withdrawal limitations. Please see a Signature professional if you have any additional questions regarding your money market account. ↵

[2] Monogram Money Market Funds Program Accounts are: • Not FDIC Insured • May Lose Value • No Bank Guarantee ↵

[3] A withdrawal is deemed to be made on the date it is posted to your account, which is not necessarily the date you initiated the withdrawal. Except for the fee for exceeding eight withdrawals during a monthly statement cycle, there is no fee charged by Signature for accessing your account at an ATM, POS terminal or other electronic facility. ↵
